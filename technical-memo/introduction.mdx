## 1.1 How to Read This Technical Memorandum

This Technical Memorandum provides a detailed explanation of the workings of the Fidivio SaaS tool that was developed to support operators of loyalty programs in their accounting, and financial planning and analysis tasks.

The memorandum covers:

- The components and terminology used in Fidivio
- The four configuration parameters and their options
- The model inputs
- The detailed calculations underlying each output (Deferred Revenue Dashboard, IFRS P&L, Adjusted EBITDA P&L, Reconciliation)
- Background accounting guidance on standalone selling prices, contract liability, marketing income, and breakage income
- Miscellaneous accounting considerations such as Tier Status benefits
