---
title: "Components Used in Fidivio"
description: "Definitions of all terms and components used in Fidivio, with the section of the platform each is used in."
---

This section provides an overview of all terms used in Fidivio. It also indicates what section of Fidivio each component is used in.

| Fidivio Component | What Section Is It Used? | What Is the Definition? |
|---|---|---|
| **Adjusted EBITDA** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L | Measurement of profitability on the basis of Gross Billings, redemption costs and potential adjustments for the period |
| **Adjustment for Change in Breakage** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L | Applies a true-up in the Adjusted EBITDA P&L to reflect a new outlook on breakage in relation to the outstanding points |
| **Adjustment for Change in Cost Per Point** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L | Applies a true-up in the Adjusted EBITDA P&L to reflect a new cost per point for the points expected to be redeemed |
| **Adjustment for Future Redemption** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L | Provision or release of Gross Billings to reflect actual burn volumes in a period versus the long-term outlook |
| **Adjustment for Liability True Up** | IFRS P&L | Applies a true-up in the IFRS P&L to reflect the impact of a change in breakage and/or the standalone selling price (either manual or automatic depending on the setting of Parameter 2) |
| **Adjustment to Deferred Revenue** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | — |
| **Standalone Selling Price / Applied_Standalone Selling Price** | IFRS P&L | The standalone selling price per point. Represents the fair value of the future performance obligation per point. Depending on the choice setting of Parameter 4, it is adjusted for breakage or not. |
| **Breakage Adjustment** | Reconciliation between Adjusted EBITDA and IFRS Profit | — |
| **Breakage Level** | Management KPIs, Adjusted EBITDA and IFRS Profit | Management estimate of portion of points that will ultimately not be redeemed |
| **Change in Deferred Revenue** | Deferred Revenue dashboard, Reconciliation between Adjusted EBITDA and IFRS Profit | — |
| **Closing Balance (points)** | Points Movement | Number of points outstanding in the system at the end of a period |
| **Closing Balance At The End Of The Period** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Amount of contractual liability in the form of deferred revenue (unearned revenue) at the end of a period |
| **Cost Per Point** | Deferred Revenue dashboard, Reconciliation between Adjusted EBITDA and IFRS Profit | Per point equivalent of redemption costs |
| **Cost Per Point Adjustment** | Reconciliation between Adjusted EBITDA and IFRS Profit | — |
| **Cost Per Point (Latest Experienced)** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Per point equivalent of redemption costs for the latest period |
| **Deferred Revenue Per Point** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | The amount of deferred revenue attributable to each outstanding loyalty point at the end of the period |
| **EBITDA** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L | Total Gross Billings minus total costs for the period |
| **Future Redemption Adjustment** | Reconciliation between Adjusted EBITDA and IFRS Profit | — |
| **Gross Billings** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L | Proceeds from sale of points |
| **IFRS Profit** | IFRS P&L | Measurement of profitability on the basis of the accounting principles |
| **Liability Balance Before Revenue Recognition** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Opening balance of deferred revenue plus deferred revenue created during the period |
| **Liability Created** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Deferred revenue created during the period |
| **Marketing Income** | IFRS P&L | Difference between the yield per point (consideration received) and standalone selling price per point |
| **Non-Point Revenue** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L and IFRS P&L | Revenues generated from other products and services |
| **Opening Balance (Points)** | Points Movement | Number of points outstanding in the system at the start of a period |
| **Opening Balance At The Start Of The Period** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Amount of contract liability in the form of deferred revenue at the start of a period |
| **Points Adjustments** | Points Movement | Breakage Reconciliation. This input is only required when Parameter 3 is set as 2 |
| **Points Earned** | Points Movement | Number of points issued during the period |
| **Points Expired** | Points Movement | Number of points expired during the period |
| **Points Issuance** | Management KPIs | Number of points issued during the period |
| **Points Redeemed** | Management KPIs | Number of points redeemed during the period |
| **Potential Adjustments** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L | Sum of the three potential adjustments that are part of the Adjusted EBITDA calculations (for redemption pattern, cost per point and/or breakage changes) |
| **Profit Margin** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L and IFRS P&L | Expresses the level of profitability by comparing profit delivered over revenues or gross billings in the period |
| **Program Running Cost** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L and IFRS P&L | All costs other than redemption costs incurred during the period |
| **Redemption Cost** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L and IFRS P&L | All cost of redemptions during the period |
| **Revenue per Point (rev_per_point)** | IFRS P&L | Derived each period. Equals the total contract liability balance before recognition divided by the total points in circulation. As redemptions occur and the liability is released, this rate evolves over time. |
| **Revenue Recognised (from Redemptions)** | IFRS P&L | Part of the deferred revenue which is recognized during the period as redemptions materialize |
| **Revenue Recognized (from Breakage)** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Revenue recognized in conjunction with points ultimately not being redeemed using one of three potential protocols under Parameter 3 (in proportion to breakage; based on validity; or based on actual expiry) |
| **Total Cost** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L and IFRS P&L | Redemption costs and all other costs for the period |
| **Total Deferred Revenue** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Amount of contractual liability in the form of deferred revenue (unearned revenue) at the end of a period |
| **Total Points Balance** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Number of points outstanding in the system at the end of a period |
| **Total Points Expected not to be Redeemed** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Number of points out of the Total Points balance that are projected not to be redeemed (using the latest breakage estimate) |
| **Total Points Expected to be Redeemed** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Number of points out of the Total Points balance that are projected to be redeemed (using the latest breakage estimate) |
| **Total points in circulation** | Deferred Revenue dashboard | Opening points balance plus points issued in a period |
| **Total Points Redeemed** | Homepage – Accounting section | Total number of points redeemed since inception of the program |
| **Total Revenue** | Adjusted EBITDA P&L and IFRS P&L | Total revenues (from points and non-points) |
| **Yield Per Point** | Management KPIs | Average selling price per point for the period. Price at which the programme sells points to partners (e.g. a bank or airline). Direct input. |
