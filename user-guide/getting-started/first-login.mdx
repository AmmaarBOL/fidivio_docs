---

Access Fidivio through your web browser using the credentials provided by your administrator.

## First-time sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login page">
    Go to the Fidivio login page in your web browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter credentials">
    Enter your registered email address and password, then click **Login**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up 2FA">
    Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or similar).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your app to complete setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Fidivio login and 2FA setup">
  ![Login and 2FA setup](/images/user-guide/getting-started/login.png)
</Frame>

## Subsequent sign ins

1. Enter your email and password, then click **Login**.
2. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app when prompted.

## Password and 2FA recovery

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Forgot password">
    Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen. A reset link is sent to your registered email within a few minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lost 2FA device">
    Contact your Administrator to reset your 2FA. See [2FA reset](/user-guide/troubleshooting/2fa-reset) for admin steps.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
If you forget your password, click **Forgot Password** on the login screen. If you have lost access to your 2FA device, contact your Administrator to reset your 2FA.
</Info>
