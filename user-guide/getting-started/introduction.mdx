## What you'll learn

This guide is organized around your workflow — not backend modules. You'll move from understanding the platform to configuring it, uploading data, and reviewing results.

| Section | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| Getting Started | Login, first-time setup, and your initial checklist |
| Platform Navigation | Dashboards, growth tools, and setup areas |
