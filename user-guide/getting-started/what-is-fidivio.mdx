Fidivio is a SaaS platform that helps loyalty program operators manage accounting, financial reporting, and planning — without building models from scratch.

## Who it's for

Fidivio is designed for finance teams, program operators, and analysts who need:

- IFRS 15 / GAAP-compliant revenue recognition for loyalty points
- Clear visibility into deferred revenue and contract liability
- Operational KPIs alongside financial statements

## Core capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Revenue recognition & liability">
    Manage deferred revenue and contract liability accounting under IFRS 15 and GAAP. Track points issuance, redemption, breakage, and expiry with configurable recognition logic.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Financial statements">
    Produce IFRS / GAAP P&L statements, Adjusted EBITDA P&L statements, and reconciliation between the two views.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Forward-looking analysis">
    Model projections and scenarios for points issuance, redemption, and financial outcomes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Program administration">
    Manage partners, users, roles, and data uploads from a single platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What Fidivio helps you do

- Manage deferred revenue and contract liability under IFRS 15 / GAAP
- Produce IFRS / GAAP P&L and Adjusted EBITDA P&L statements
- Reconcile between the two P&L views
- Track points issuance, redemption, breakage, and expiry
- Model forward-looking projections and scenarios
- Manage partners, users, and roles
