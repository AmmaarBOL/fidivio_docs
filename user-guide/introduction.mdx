**Fidivio** is the world's first off-the-shelf financial analysis tool dedicated to loyalty programs. It supports loyalty program operators in their accounting, and financial planning and analysis tasks.

This User Guide covers everything you need to get started, navigate the platform, configure your program, and troubleshoot common issues.

## What Fidivio Does

Fidivio is a SaaS tool that enables loyalty program operators to:

- Manage deferred revenue and contract liability accounting under IFRS 15 / GAAP
- Produce IFRS / GAAP P&L statements
- Produce Adjusted EBITDA P&L statements
- Reconcile between the two P&L views
- Track points issuance, redemption, breakage, and expiry
- Model forward-looking projections and scenarios
- Manage partners, users, and roles
