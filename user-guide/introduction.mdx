## What is Fidivio?

Fidivio is a next-generation **loyalty and rewards management platform** designed to help businesses build meaningful, long-lasting customer relationships. Powered by the Loyalty Juggernaut engine, Fidivio provides a comprehensive suite of tools to design, deploy, and manage loyalty programs at scale.

## Who is Fidivio For?

Fidivio serves a wide range of industries and business sizes:

- **Retail & E-commerce** — Drive repeat purchases and increase basket size
- **Hospitality & Travel** — Build long-term guest loyalty across properties and partners
- **Financial Services** — Reward cardholders and deepen wallet share
- **Fuel & Convenience** — Capture frequent transactions with compelling rewards
- **Coalition Networks** — Manage multi-partner earn-and-burn programs

## Platform Architecture

Fidivio is built on a **cloud-native, API-first architecture** that supports:

- High-throughput transaction processing
- Multi-tenant program management
- Real-time event streaming
- Flexible rule engine for business logic
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance
