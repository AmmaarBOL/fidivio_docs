## Forgot password

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login screen.
2. Check your registered email for a reset link (allow a few minutes).
3. Check spam or junk folders if the email does not arrive.

## Cannot log in after password reset

- Ensure you completed the reset link process fully.
- Clear browser cache or try an incognito window.
- Contact your Administrator if the account may be deactivated.

## Account deactivated

Deactivated users cannot log in. Ask your Administrator to reactivate the account under **User Management**.

## Invitation email not received

<Info>
Invitation emails are sent from a Fidivio system address. Check spam/junk folders. Administrators can verify the email address in User Management.
</Info>
