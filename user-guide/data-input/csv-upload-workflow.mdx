Follow this workflow for every batch upload. It applies to all seven data categories.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare your data">
    Confirm partner codes exist, accounting settings are finalized, and source data is reconciled for the period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the CSV template">
    In the relevant data category, click **+ Add → Upload** and download the template for that category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Populate the file">
    Fill in rows using `insert` for new records. Leave `id` blank for inserts. See [CSV specifications](/user-guide/reference/csv-specifications).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the file">
    Upload the completed CSV via **Add → Upload**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate the upload">
    Go to the **Batches** tab and confirm status is **Success**. See [Upload status](/user-guide/data-input/upload-status).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fix errors if needed">
    If status is **Failed with Errors**, download the handback file, correct rows, and re-upload. See [Error handling](/user-guide/data-input/error-handling).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="CSV upload workflow">
  ![CSV upload](/images/user-guide/data-input/csv-upload-workflow.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
Upload one category at a time for your first migration. Validate each category on dashboards before proceeding to the next.
</Tip>

<Warning>
Category-specific values like `revenue_category`, `cost_category`, and partner codes must be pre-configured before batch upload. They cannot be created during CSV import.
</Warning>
