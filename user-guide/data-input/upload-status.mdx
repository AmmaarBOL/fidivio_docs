---
title: "Upload status"
description: "Monitor batch upload processing status in Fidivio."
---

After submitting a batch upload, monitor processing on the **Batches** tab without leaving the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the data category">
    Navigate to the relevant category (e.g. Accruals, Redemptions).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Batches tab">
    Click **Batches** at the top of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your upload">
    Locate your file by submission date, record count, and status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review details">
    Click the **view (eye) icon** for success/failure breakdown and handback file download.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Status values

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Processing** | Upload is being validated and ingested |
| **Success** | All records imported successfully |
| **Failed with Errors** | One or more records could not be imported |

<Tip>
Always check the Batches tab after a large upload before proceeding to view reports.
</Tip>
