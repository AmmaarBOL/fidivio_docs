The three accounting settings — Applied SSP, Breakage Recognition, and Deferred Revenue True-up — work together to determine the full IFRS income statement.

## Income statement impact

| Income Statement Line | Driven By |
| --- | --- |
| **Revenue recognized (redemptions)** | All 3 settings. Applied SSP, Breakage Recognition, and Deferred Revenue True-up all influence the effective revenue rate per point recognised. |
| **Breakage income** | Timing controlled by Breakage Recognition. Revenue per point also influenced by Applied SSP and Deferred Revenue True-up. |
| **Marketing income** | Driven by Applied SSP. Arises when yield per point exceeds applied SSP. Higher when SSP is post-breakage (Yes). Does not apply to conventional model programs. |
| **Adjustment for liability true-up** | Driven exclusively by Deferred Revenue True-up. Zero when Setting 1 (no true-up) is selected. |
