Each dashboard in Fidivio answers a specific financial or operational question. Use this guide to find the right view for your analysis.

## Accounting dashboards

| Sub-section | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **IFRS Dashboard** | Detailed financial breakdown behind the IFRS P&L |
| **Deferred Revenue Dashboard** | Movements in deferred revenue and contract liability |
| **Points Movement Dashboard** | Points earned, redeemed, expired, and adjusted in the selected period |

## Financial Statements

| Sub-section | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Income Statement** | IFRS P&L, Adjusted EBITDA P&L, and reconciliation between the two |

## Management Reports

| Sub-section | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **KPI Dashboard** | Key operational KPIs for the program |
| **A-EBITDA Waterfall** | Financial breakdown behind the Adjusted EBITDA P&L |
| **Partner Dashboard** | Points and financial KPIs by partner |
| **Sankey Chart** | Visual flow from Gross Billings to key financial components |
| **Partner Margin** | Points status, redemption composition, and margin by partner |

<Tip>
After uploading data, check the Deferred Revenue Dashboard first to validate liability movements before reviewing P&L lines.
</Tip>
