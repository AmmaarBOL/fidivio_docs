The left-hand sidebar is your primary navigation. The **Dashboards** section provides high-level and detailed views of your program's financial and operational performance.

## Dashboard menu

| Menu item | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Home** | High-level overview across Accounting, Financial Statements, and Management Reports |
| **Accounting** | IFRS, Deferred Revenue, and Points Movement dashboards |
| **Financial Statements** | Income Statement with IFRS P&L, Adjusted EBITDA, and reconciliation |
| **Management Reports** | KPI, A-EBITDA Waterfall, Partner, Sankey, and Partner Margin views |
