Fidivio's IFRS income statement is driven by three configuration settings working together with your uploaded data.

## Revenue lines

| Line | Primary driver |
| --- | --- |
| Revenue recognized (redemptions) | Applied SSP + Breakage Recognition + True-up |
| Breakage income | Breakage Recognition timing |
| Marketing income | Applied SSP (profit-centre programs only) |
| Adjustment for liability true-up | Deferred Revenue True-up setting |

## Configuration flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Points issued">
    Accruals and Breakage Levels data drive initial deferral at applied SSP.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redemptions">
    Revenue recognized per point based on configuration and evolving liability balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Breakage release">
    Breakage income timing follows your Breakage Recognition setting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Estimate changes">
    True-up setting determines whether catch-up adjustments hit the P&L.
  </Step>
</Steps>
