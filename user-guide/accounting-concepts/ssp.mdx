title: "Standalone selling price (SSP)"
description: "What SSP means in Fidivio and how it affects revenue recognition."
---

The Standalone Selling Price (SSP) of a loyalty point is what the program operator would charge if selling that point separately to a customer.

In Fidivio, the SSP data input is the gross face value of one point, before adjustment for expected breakage.

## Key concepts

- **SSP input** — Face value entered in Breakage Levels data
- **Applied SSP** — Value used in calculations, which may or may not be adjusted for breakage depending on your [configuration](/user-guide/program-setup/applied-ssp)

## Why it matters

SSP determines how much revenue is deferred as contract liability versus recognised on the income statement. It also affects marketing income for profit-centre programs.
