---
title: "Breakage recognition"
description: "How Fidivio recognizes revenue from points members are not expected to redeem."
---

Breakage is the portion of loyalty points that members are not expected to redeem. Fidivio offers three recognition methods aligned with IFRS 15 practice.

## Recognition methods

| Method | Summary |
| --- | --- |
| **Proportional to redemption (default)** | Breakage released as redemptions occur |
| **Proportional to validity period** | Breakage spread evenly over point validity window |
| **On actual expiry** | Breakage recognised only when points expire |

<Note>
Breakage income should be released progressively so the liability balance reflects the remaining obligation.
</Note>

## Related configuration

Configure the method under [Breakage recognition setting](/user-guide/program-setup/breakage-recognition). Enter assumptions in [Breakage levels](/user-guide/data-categories/breakage-levels) and [Expiry](/user-guide/data-categories/expiry) data.
