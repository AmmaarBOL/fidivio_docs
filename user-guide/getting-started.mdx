# Getting Started with Fidivio

This guide walks you through the initial setup of your Fidivio account and helps you configure your first loyalty program.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A Fidivio account (contact [sales@fidivio.com](mailto:sales@fidivio.com) to get started)
- Administrator credentials for your organization
- Basic understanding of your loyalty program goals

## Step 1: Log In to Fidivio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Platform">
    Navigate to your Fidivio instance URL provided during onboarding (e.g., `https://your-org.fidivio.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Enter your administrator email and password. For first-time login, use the temporary credentials sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Your Password">
    On first login, you will be prompted to set a new secure password. Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure MFA (Recommended)">
    Enable Multi-Factor Authentication under **Settings \> Security** for enhanced account protection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Configure Your Organization

After login, navigate to **Settings \> Organization** to configure:

| Setting | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Organization Name | Your brand name as displayed to members |
| Base Currency | Primary currency for program transactions |
| Default Language | Platform and member-facing language |
| Time Zone | Used for campaign scheduling and reporting |
| Program Type | Points-based, tier-based, or hybrid |

## Step 3: Set Up Your Loyalty Program

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Programs">
    Go to **Loyalty \> Programs** and click **Create New Program**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Program Basics">
    Set the program name, description, start date, and earning structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Earning Rules">
    Define how members earn points — per transaction value, per item, or per event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Redemption">
    Configure how members can redeem rewards — vouchers, discounts, products, or partner rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Tiers (Optional)">
    Create membership tiers (e.g., Silver, Gold, Platinum) with qualification criteria and tier benefits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Program">
    Review your configuration and click **Publish** to make the program live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 5: Enroll Your First Members

You can enroll members through:

- **Bulk Import**: Upload a CSV file via **Members \> Import**
- **API Enrollment**: Use the Member Enrollment API
- **Self-Registration**: Enable member self-signup on your website or app
- **In-Store Enrollment**: Register members at POS terminals

<Note>
  After your first member is enrolled, they will appear in **Members \> All Members** and start accumulating points on qualifying transactions.
</Note>
