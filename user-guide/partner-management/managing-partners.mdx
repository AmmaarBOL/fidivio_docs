---
title: "Managing partners"
description: "Add, edit, and manage partner organisations in Fidivio."
---

Partners are external organisations that issue and/or redeem points within your loyalty program — airlines, retailers, hotels, or financial services companies.

Each partner must be configured before any data referencing that partner can be uploaded.

**Location:** Setup & Support → Partners

## Add a new partner

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Partners">
    Go to **Setup & Support → Partners**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Partner">
    Click **+ Add Partner** in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details">
    Complete the required fields (see [Partner configuration](/user-guide/partner-management/partner-configuration)).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The partner appears in the list and in Data Input dropdowns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Partners management screen">
  ![Partners screen](/images/user-guide/partner-management/managing-partners.png)
</Frame>

## Edit partner details

1. On the Partners screen, click the **edit (pen) icon** on the partner's row.
2. Update the relevant fields.
3. Click **Save**.

<Tip>
Establish a naming convention for partner codes before uploading data. Codes cannot be changed retroactively without re-uploading affected records.
</Tip>

<Warning>
CSV batch uploads referencing an Inactive partner's code will be rejected. Set the partner to Active before uploading, then revert if needed.
</Warning>
